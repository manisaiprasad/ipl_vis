IPL (Indian Premier League)


Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India contested during March or April and May of every year by eight teams representing eight different cities in India. The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2008.

About Project

In this project we have used a IPL dataset consists of matches and deliveries. We have used the data to perform data preprocessing with Pandas. Performed various data analysis on the dataset and created a dashboard with all visualizations (like bar chart, pie chart and other) and extracted some data insights.
